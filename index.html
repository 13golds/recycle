Welcome to Recycle - A Green Future with Cryptocurrency

What is Recycle?

**Recycle** is a cryptocurrency designed to motivate recycling by rewarding users with tokens for their waste recycling efforts. This system aims to connect individuals, recycling plants, and organizations to build a more sustainable planet.

How it Works

Users earn **Recycle Tokens** by participating in the recycling program. Tokens are then used to reward participants and incentivize businesses and municipalities to adopt eco-friendly practices.

Our Partners

Our partners include recycling plants, eco-conscious companies, and governments working together to create an effective recycling system powered by blockchain and cryptocurrency.
